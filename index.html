Welcome to ChessPresso!

Your daily early morning shot of Chess!

Chesspresso is your basic begginner-friendly chess website. The primary goal of this website is to get the non-players inspired and excited towards the game of chess as chess is beneficial to everyone.

You can use this website to learn about the history and origin of chess, learn about the rules of chess and how to play a game. You can also play a game with your friend or against the computer to get comfortable with the rules and practice the game. Since the target users are beginners, the level of difficulty for the computer is pretty low, and anyone with a basic knowledge of the rules should be able to beat the computer pretty comfortably.

Finally, this website can also be used to view the all-time top players of the game, and their FIDE ratings, so as to give new players an idea of how high they can achieve. For beginner players, the approximate FIDE rating would be 500.
